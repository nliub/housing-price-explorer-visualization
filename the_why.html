Why should I a homeowner?

Real estate has historically been a stable investment. The fastest growing 200 regions in our analysis grew on average over >14% year-over-year from 2017 to now. The top grwoing region was able to quadruple in value in just 6 years.

Protection Against Inflation

The most simple definition of inflation is that money decreases in value over time compared to goods and services. Being a home owner puts you in a better place because that means your house, since it is something tangible, adds value compared to when you hold money in your hands.
Take a look at the graph below: median home prices per square feet have grown steadily over the past 6 years; the year-over-year (YoY) growth rates have remained positive.
Even taking inflation into consideration, the net worth of your home is guaranteed to increase over time.
